1

Get Wikipedia Updated to Reflect 2026 Campaign

High Priority Timeline: 3-7 days for response, 2-4 weeks for AI propagation
Problem:

Your Wikipedia article says you're a "former" congressman with no mention of the 2026 NJ-11 special election. AI systems pull heavily from Wikipedia—this outdated info is why ChatGPT fails 80% of the time.

⚠️ Important: Wikipedia Conflict of Interest Policy

Candidates and campaign staff should NOT directly edit their own Wikipedia pages. Wikipedia has strict COI policies. Edits by campaigns are often reverted. Use these approved methods instead:

Option A: Request Edit via Talk Page (Recommended)

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Talk:Tom_Malinowski

Post a polite request asking volunteer editors to update the article. Be transparent that you're affiliated with the campaign. Provide reliable sources (news articles) that editors can verify.

Sample Talk Page Post:

Hello, I'm writing on behalf of the Malinowski campaign (declaring COI per Wikipedia policy). The article doesn't reflect that Tom Malinowski is currently a candidate in the 2026 NJ-11 special election. Here are reliable sources that could be used to update the article:

• [NJ.com article about candidacy announcement]
• [Press release about Morris County Democratic Committee endorsement]
• [NJ Globe coverage of the special election]

Would an uninvolved editor be willing to add this information? Thank you.

What the Article Needs (for whoever edits it):

In the introduction: Add that he is "currently a candidate in the 2026 special election for New Jersey's 11th congressional district."

New section "2026 congressional campaign":
• Announcement of candidacy for NJ-11 special election
• Endorsement from U.S. Senator Andy Kim (D-NJ)
• Morris County Democratic Committee endorsement (after 9 rounds of RCV)
• J Street PAC endorsement
• Special election date: February 11, 2026
• Campaign themes: lowering costs, protecting democracy, fighting corruption

Key sources to cite: NJ Globe, InsiderNJ, NJ.com (not campaign press releases alone)
Expected Impact:

Wikipedia updates propagate to AI systems within 2-4 weeks. The Talk page approach usually gets response within 3-7 days. Could improve ChatGPT accuracy from 20% to 80%+.

2

Claim & Update Google Knowledge Panel

High Priority Timeline: 1-3 weeks
Problem:

Your Google Knowledge Panel shows outdated information. It lists you as "Former U.S. Representative" without mentioning your current candidacy for NJ-11.

Step 1: Claim Your Knowledge Panel

https://support.google.com/knowledgepanel/answer/7534842

Search for "Tom Malinowski" on Google, click the Knowledge Panel, then click "Claim this knowledge panel" at the bottom. You'll need to verify your identity.

Step 2: Submit a Suggested Edit

https://support.google.com/knowledgepanel/answer/7534916
Current: "Tom Malinowski is an American politician and diplomat who served as U.S. Representative for New Jersey's 7th congressional district from 2019 to 2023."

Suggested Change: "Tom Malinowski is an American politician and diplomat. He served as U.S. Representative for New Jersey's 7th congressional district from 2019 to 2023 and is currently the Democratic candidate in the 2026 special election for New Jersey's 11th congressional district."
Expected Impact:

Knowledge Panel updates can take 1-3 weeks. Google reviews all edits manually. Submit with reliable sources (news articles about your candidacy).

5

Address Stock Trading Controversy

Critical Timeline: Ongoing
Problem:

The 2021-2022 stock trading ethics investigation appears in 30% of search results. AI systems mention it when asked about controversies without providing your side of the story.

Recommended Approach

This cannot be removed from results, but can be contextualized. Ensure your campaign website has a clear statement addressing this issue, linking to the ethics committee's resolution. When AI systems find your direct statement, they present both sides rather than just the accusation.

Suggested Website Statement (Issues or About page):

On Financial Disclosure: In 2022, the House Ethics Committee reviewed stock trades made by my financial advisor and found no evidence of wrongdoing. I support the STOCK Act and have advocated for stronger congressional trading restrictions. [Link to ethics committee resolution if public]
Expected Impact:

Having your own statement on the record means AI systems can present it alongside any controversy mentions, rather than only presenting the accusation.
