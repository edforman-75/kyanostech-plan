Key Insights

Way has massive outside support - DLGA spending $670K+ (TV + mail), dwarfing all other candidates combined. This is 6x more than all digital spending in the race.
Malinowski is the Super PAC favorite on digital - United Democracy Project ($353K reserved) + The 218 Project (~$100K) = ~$450K in outside digital support.
Gill is self-funding his digital - Highest direct campaign digital spend ($7,500-$8,500) but no Super PAC support. Relying on party machine endorsements.
Mejia consolidated progressive endorsements - Bernie Sanders, AOC, Progressive Caucus PAC, Working Families Party. No major Super PAC spending yet.
Platform arbitrage opportunity - Gill absent from Google entirely. Malinowski owns Google but underweight on Meta. Cross-platform strategy could be a differentiator.
