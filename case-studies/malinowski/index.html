The Bottom Line

Across all six monitoring channels, the campaign is losing the information war. AI platforms—which research shows are 4x more persuasive than TV ads—lead with ethics violations. Social media has 256K hostile views going unanswered. Opponents are spending on attack ads while the campaign's positive press coverage isn't even on its own website.

The two highest-impact channels are the most broken: Google AI Overview (D) doesn't know there's a 2026 campaign, and ChatGPT (C) leads every trust question with stock trading violations. These are the channels voters encounter when actively researching—the moment of highest persuasion potential.

The good news: most of this is fixable. AI systems cite the sources they can find. Create an ethics FAQ page, and AI will cite it. Add press coverage to the website, and AI will find fresh content. Respond to YouTube attacks, and the 480:1 hostile-to-positive ratio starts to shift. The campaign controls the inputs that determine what voters see.

Key Findings

!

Google AI Overview doesn't mention the current campaign

The AI summary at the top of Google Search only shows "served from 2019 to 2023." No mention of NJ-11 or the February 2026 election. Voters searching don't know he's running.

!

ChatGPT leads with ethics violations on trust questions

When voters ask "Is Tom Malinowski honest?", ChatGPT opens with STOCK Act violations before mentioning his diplomatic career or the blind trust resolution.

~

Campaign website has no counter-narrative

No ethics FAQ page, no endorsements page, no immigration page despite the compelling refugee story. AI has no authoritative campaign source to cite on these topics.

~

News section last updated April 2020

5 positive articles from the past 60 days aren't on the website. AI systems looking for fresh content find only the controversy.

✓

Human rights credentials work across all platforms

Human Rights Watch and State Department service surface consistently. This narrative works because it has source consistency—apply this model to other topics.

!

YouTube attack ads have 256K views—unanswered

The 218 Project is running attack ads with more reach than any campaign content. Bernie Sanders endorsed Mejia with 16K video views. Progressive energy consolidating against establishment.
