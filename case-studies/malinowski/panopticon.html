As someone who fled Communist Poland at age six and grew up in New Jersey as an immigrant, Tom understands firsthand what it means to seek a better life in America.

During his time as Assistant Secretary of State for Democracy, Human Rights, and Labor, Tom worked on refugee resettlement and asylum policy. At Human Rights Watch, he advocated for humane treatment of migrants worldwide.

Tom's immigration priorities:
- Protect Dreamers and create a path to citizenship for DACA recipients
- Reform the legal immigration system to reduce backlogs
- Secure the border while treating migrants with dignity
- Support refugee resettlement consistent with American values
