Research Note: Traditional Contact

Academic research (Kalla & Broockman, 2018) found that traditional campaign contact has near-zero persuasion effect in general elections. However, persuasion does work in low-information environments—like primaries and down-ballot races—where voters are actively seeking information. This is exactly when AI provides first impressions.

New Research: AI Is 4x More Persuasive Than Ads

Landmark studies published in Nature and Science (December 2025) by MIT's David Rand and Cornell's Gordon Pennycook found that AI chatbots are approximately 4 times more effective at political persuasion than traditional advertising.

In the 2024 U.S. election, a pro-Harris AI chatbot moved Trump supporters 3.9 points on a 100-point scale—roughly 4x the measured effect of political ads in 2016 and 2020. In Canadian and Polish elections, AI achieved shifts of ~10 percentage points among opposition voters.

The implication: AI isn't just another channel—it may be the most persuasive political communication medium ever studied. When voters encounter AI responses about candidates, those responses have outsized influence on their opinions.

Note: The costs above for TV, CTV, digital, and mail are media placement only. They do not include production costs (creating the ads) or the standard 15% commission to the media buyer of record. True all-in costs for traditional advertising are 20-30% higher than shown.
