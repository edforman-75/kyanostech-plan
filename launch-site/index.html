Common Questions

Is this legal?

Yes. We query AI platforms as any user would and analyze the responses. There's no hacking, no terms of service violations, and no manipulation of AI systems.

Why is the trial free?

We're building the definitive platform for AI monitoring in politics. Your feedback and real-world usage data are invaluable for refining our tools before the 2026 cycle.

What if AI says something wrong about my candidate?

We'll alert you immediately. Our reports include specific recommendations: update your website, issue a statement, contact the platform, or simply monitor for changes.

How is this different from media monitoring?

Traditional media monitoring tracks news coverage. AI monitoring tracks what AI assistants tell voters directly — a new, high-influence information channel that most campaigns ignore.

Who's behind Kyanos?

Kyanos is led by political technology veterans with deep experience in Democratic campaigns and data infrastructure. We're backed by progressive investors committed to fair elections.

When does monitoring start?

Approved campaigns can begin monitoring as soon as onboarding is complete. Full monitoring typically begins 30-60 days before you want comprehensive baseline data.
