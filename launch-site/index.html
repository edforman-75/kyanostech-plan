Common Questions

Is this legal?

Yes. We query internet platforms—chatbots and search—as any user would and analyze the responses. There's no hacking, no terms of service violations, and no manipulation of AI systems.

Why is the trial free?

We're building the definitive platform for AI monitoring in politics. Your feedback and real-world usage data are invaluable for refining our tools before the 2028 cycle.

What if AI says something wrong about my candidate?

We'll alert you immediately. Our reports include specific recommendations: update your website, issue a statement, contact the platform, or simply monitor for changes.

How is this different from media monitoring?

Traditional media monitoring tracks news coverage. We track what AI assistants tell voters directly, plus opponent websites, political ads, and social media—high-influence channels that most campaigns ignore.

Who's behind Kyanos?

Kyanos is led by political technology veterans with deep experience in progressive campaigns and data infrastructure. We're backed by progressive investors committed to fair elections.

When does monitoring start?

Approved campaigns can begin monitoring as soon as onboarding is complete.
