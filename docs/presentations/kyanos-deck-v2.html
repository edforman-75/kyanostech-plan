Why This Works

The Causal Chain

  • Campaign creates content (website, press, social)
  • Search engines index and assign authority
  • AI retrieves and synthesizes from indexed sources
  • Voter sees AI response as first impression

Key insight: Campaigns already control the most authoritative sourcetheir own website. They just don't know what to put on it.

The Arbitrage

Traditional persuasion:
$50-200 per voter moved

AI presence optimization:
$2-5 per voter reached at moment of research

Not a replacement for paid media. A force multiplier that makes every dollar work harder.
