# Investment Requirements

## Capital Plan

KyanosTech requires **$5.0 million in total investment** to execute the 2025-2028 strategy:

### Funding Rounds

- **Seed Round: $500,000** (2025)
  - Product development and initial market validation
  - Team assembly and relationship building
  - Technology infrastructure setup

- **Series A: $2.5 million** (2026)
  - Scale pilot deployments
  - Expand sales and marketing
  - Build out platform integrations

- **Convertible Debt: $2.0 million** (2027-2028)
  - Bridge to profitability
  - Scale customer acquisition
  - Ensure operational runway through 2028 cycle

### Use of Funds

The investment enables:
- Product development of AGON, POLIS, and SCOPE platforms
- Sales and marketing to reach 259 customers by 2028
- Partnership development with Democratic digital ecosystem
- Infrastructure for schema standards and bias monitoring
- Working capital to reach break-even in Month 36

### Investment Structure

KyanosTech is structured as a **B Corporation**, offering:
- **Downside protection**: Tax-deductible losses if unsuccessful
- **Upside participation**: Capital recovery and moderate returns on success
- **Mission alignment**: Impact-driven returns tied to Democratic visibility

### Timeline

- **2025 (Seed)**: Relationship building, no revenue
- **2026 (Series A)**: Pilot deployments, early revenue
- **2027-2028 (Convertible)**: Scale to $6.4M revenue, 259 customers

The capital plan is designed to reach break-even by Month 36 while maintaining a minimum reserve buffer of $0.5M for risk mitigation.
